world_gdp.csv:
  Downloaded GDP per capita in USD of 1960-2018 
  from World Bank website on March 23 2020.
  According to the original file: 
    "Data Source"="World Development Indicators",
    "Last Updated Date","2020-03-18",
  World Bank: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/ny.gdp.pcap.cd?end=2018&most_recent_value_desc=true&start=2018 


us_population.csv:
  Downloaded annual estimates of the resident population
  from US census using a reference in wiki on March 23, 2020.
  Here, first 2 columns (Census, and Estimates Base) are from 2010 Census on April 1, 2010.
  Next columns with years represnts estimated population as of July 1.
  Wiki ref: "Annual Estimates of the Resident Population for the United States, Regions,  States, and Puerto Rico: April 1, 2010 to July 1, 2019". 2010-2019 Population Estimates. United States Census Bureau, Population Division. December 30, 2019. Retrieved January 27, 2020. https://www2.census.gov/programs-surveys/popest/tables/2010-2019/state/totals/nst-est2019-01.xlsx?#
